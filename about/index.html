About HowLongDrive

HowLongDrive.com is a free road trip planning tool that helps you find driving times, distances, and trip costs between cities across the United States.

🚗 What We Offer

  • Driving Times - Accurate drive times between 300+ city pairs
  • Distance Calculator - Miles and kilometers for every route
  • Gas Cost Estimates - Based on current fuel prices and fuel efficiency
  • Toll Estimates - Approximate toll costs for major routes
  • Best Times to Drive - Avoid traffic with our timing recommendations

⚡ EV Trip Planner

Planning a road trip in an electric vehicle? Our EV Trip Planner helps you:

  • Calculate charging stops needed based on 250-mile range
  • Estimate total trip time including charging
  • Compare EV costs vs. gas vehicle costs
  • See how much you'll save driving electric

📊 Our Data

Driving times are based on typical highway speeds and normal traffic conditions. Actual times may vary based on traffic, weather, road conditions, and your driving speed.

Gas costs are calculated assuming 30 MPG average fuel efficiency and $3.50/gallon gas price. EV costs assume $0.04/mile for electricity.
