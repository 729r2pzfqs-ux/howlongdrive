US Road Trip Guides

Plan your American adventure with our comprehensive driving guides. From coast-to-coast journeys to scenic highways, we've got every route covered.

Coast to Coast: New York to Los Angeles

2,775 miles · ~41 hours driving · The classic American road trip

Drive across America from the Atlantic to the Pacific. This iconic journey takes you through diverse landscapes, from the Appalachians to the Great Plains to the Rockies.

Full Route NY → Chicago Chicago → Denver Denver → Las Vegas Las Vegas → LA

Pacific Coast Highway

~650 miles · ~12 hours · America's most scenic coastal drive

Drive along the stunning California coast from San Francisco to Los Angeles (or further to San Diego). One of the world's most beautiful road trips.

SF → LA SF → Monterey Monterey → Santa Barbara LA → San Diego

Route 66: Chicago to Los Angeles

2,400 miles · ~35 hours · The Mother Road

Follow the historic Route 66 from Chicago to Santa Monica. Experience classic Americana, diners, and small-town charm.

Full Route Chicago → St. Louis St. Louis → OKC OKC → Albuquerque Albuquerque → LA

Florida Road Trip

Beaches, theme parks, and the Keys

Explore the Sunshine State from Miami to Orlando, or drive all the way down to Key West.

Miami → Orlando Miami → Key West Orlando → Tampa Orlando → Miami Jacksonville → Miami

Southwest National Parks

Grand Canyon, Zion, Bryce, and more

Tour the stunning national parks of the American Southwest, from the Grand Canyon to Monument Valley.

Vegas → Grand Canyon Vegas → Zion Phoenix → Grand Canyon Phoenix → Sedona SLC → Yellowstone

Texas Triangle

Houston, Dallas, San Antonio, Austin

Explore the major cities of Texas in one epic road trip through the Lone Star State.

Houston → Dallas Dallas → Austin Austin → San Antonio San Antonio → Houston Houston → Austin

New England Fall Foliage

Autumn colors through historic New England

Experience peak fall colors driving through Vermont, New Hampshire, and Massachusetts.

Boston → Portland Boston → Burlington NYC → Boston Boston → Cape Cod
