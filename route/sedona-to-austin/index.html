How Long Is the Drive from Sedona to Austin?

The drive from Sedona to Austin takes approximately 20h 40m, covering 1137 miles via the highway. Under normal conditions, expect to spend around $132 on gas.

Travel time can vary — during peak hours, weekends, or holidays, the journey may take 28–29+ hours. For the fastest trip, leave early morning (before 7 AM) or travel on weekday evenings.
